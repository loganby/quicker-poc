Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take? ▼

Standard delivery within the UK typically takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery options are available for urgent orders (1-2 business days). International shipping varies by location but generally takes 7-14 business days. You'll receive a tracking number with your confirmation email so you can monitor your delivery progress.

Do you ship internationally? ▼

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping costs vary based on destination and shipping method. Customs and import duties may apply depending on your country—please check with your local customs office. We partner with reliable courier services to ensure safe delivery.

What are the shipping costs? ▼

UK Standard Delivery: £3.99 (orders over £50 qualify for FREE shipping). Express Delivery: £8.99. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and weight of your order. We aim to keep shipping costs as competitive as possible.

Can I track my order? ▼

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Use this number on the courier's website to get real-time updates on your delivery status. You can also contact our support team if you have any questions about tracking.

Returns & Exchanges

What's your return policy? ▼

We offer a 30-day return policy on all purchases. If you're not completely satisfied with your shoes, you can return them in original condition with original packaging for a full refund. Simply contact our support team to initiate the return process. Return shipping is free for UK customers on most items.

How do I exchange a pair? ▼

If you need a different size or color, you can easily exchange your shoes. Simply start a return with our support team and specify the new size or color you'd like. We'll send you a replacement pair at no additional charge. Most exchanges ship within 3-5 business days.

What condition must shoes be in for returns? ▼

For returns, shoes must be unworn and in original condition with all original packaging and tags attached. If you've worn them and they show signs of wear, we may not be able to accept them for return, but we're happy to discuss any issues with fit or quality—contact our team!

How long does a refund take? ▼

Once we receive and inspect your returned items, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow 3-5 additional business days for the refund to appear in your bank account, depending on your bank.

Warranty & Quality

What warranty do Quicker shoes come with? ▼

All Quicker shoes come with a warranty covering manufacturing defects. Quickster trainers are covered for 1 year, while Quicktown formal shoes are covered for 2 years. The warranty covers issues like sole separation, faulty stitching, or material defects. Normal wear and tear are not covered. Consult your purchase confirmation for specific warranty terms.

How do I claim the warranty? ▼

If you discover a manufacturing defect, contact our support team with photos of the issue and your order number. We'll review your claim and either repair or replace the shoes at no cost. Warranty claims are typically processed within 7-10 business days.

What about durability and longevity? ▼

Quicker shoes are built to last! With proper care, most customers get 2-3 years or more of regular use from a pair. Our quality materials and rigorous manufacturing standards ensure long-lasting comfort and durability. Check our care guide on the shop page for tips on maintaining your shoes.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my correct size? ▼

Check our detailed size guide available on the shop page, which includes UK, EU, US, and foot length measurements in centimeters. For the most accurate measurement, measure your feet in the evening when they're slightly swollen, and measure from heel to toe. If you're between sizes, we recommend going up.

Do your shoes run true to size? ▼

Yes, Quicker shoes generally run true to UK sizes. However, fit can vary slightly between styles and personal preferences. Quickster trainers tend to be slightly roomier for comfort, while Quicktown formal shoes offer a snug, professional fit. Check individual product descriptions for any fit notes.

What if I'm between sizes? ▼

If you're between sizes, the choice depends on your preferences. For trainers, we recommend sizing up for extra comfort. For formal shoes, you might prefer your actual size for a more tailored fit. If you're unsure, our 30-day return policy means you can easily exchange for a different size.

Do you have half sizes? ▼

Currently, we offer full sizes from UK 6 to 13. If you typically wear a half size, we recommend the size up for comfort or size down for a snugger fit, depending on the style. Contact our team if you need personalized fit advice.

Materials & Care

What materials are used in Quicker shoes? ▼

Quickster trainers use high-quality breathable mesh and synthetic materials with cushioned soles. Quicktown formal shoes feature premium leather uppers with durable rubber or leather soles. All materials are selected for comfort, durability, and style. Check individual product pages for specific material composition.

How should I clean my shoes? ▼

For Quickster trainers: Wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap, then air dry away from heat. For Quicktown formal shoes: Use a soft brush with leather cleaner, then stuff with newspaper to maintain shape while drying. Detailed care instructions are available on our care guide table in the shop section.

How do I maintain leather shoes? ▼

For best results with leather shoes: Clean regularly with a soft brush, condition the leather monthly with quality leather conditioner, store in a cool dry place using shoe trees, and avoid direct sunlight or heat. This will keep your Quicktown shoes looking pristine for years.

Are your shoes eco-friendly? ▼

We're committed to sustainability! We're exploring and implementing eco-friendly material alternatives in our collections. Our UK manufacturing also reduces carbon footprint from transportation. Check individual product pages for sustainability information. Contact us if you have specific environmental concerns.
