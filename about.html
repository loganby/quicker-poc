Our Story

30+ years of quality, craftsmanship, and customer trust

A Legacy of Excellence

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1992, Quicker Shoes started as a small UK-based footwear manufacturer with a simple mission: to create quality shoes that stood the test of time. What began as a family operation has grown into a trusted name in premium footwear.

For over three decades, we've remained committed to British manufacturing, maintaining our production facilities in the heart of the UK. This allows us to maintain complete control over quality, from raw materials to finished product.

Our Evolution

While we started as a B2B supplier to leading retailers across Europe, we've recently made the exciting transition to Direct-to-Consumer (D2C). This shift allows us to connect directly with shoe enthusiasts who appreciate quality craftsmanship and excellent value.

Today, Quicker Shoes represents the perfect blend of heritage and innovation—traditional manufacturing expertise combined with modern design sensibilities.
